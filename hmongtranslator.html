Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/02/2025

App Name: Hmong Translator

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your information when using the app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information or user data on external servers. All data used by this app is stored locally on your device and is never shared with third parties.

2. Use of Translation Services

Our app uses a Translation API to translate text between English and Hmong. When you request a translation, the text you enter is securely sent to the Google Translation service for processing.

Audio pronunciation files are bundled within the app and are not downloaded or tracked.

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely on your device. Translation requests are transmitted securely to the Google Translation API and are not stored by us.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

5. User Consent

By using the translation features, you consent to the app sending your entered text to the Google Translation API solely for the purpose of providing translations.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and at the URL where this policy is hosted.
